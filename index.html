Our Story

From local factory to global supplier

2009

Founded in Shanghai with 20 workers, focusing on balloon decoration.

2012

Expanded to Yiwu factory, added paper decorations and tableware.

2015

First SEDEX and BSCI audits passed. Entered European retail market.

2018

FSC certification obtained. Launched eco-friendly product line.

2021

Annual output exceeded $200M. 100+ container monthly capacity.

2025

300M USD annual output, 500+ containers to global markets.
